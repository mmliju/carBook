Our Services


Interior Shampooing

Shampooing your car’s interior removes dirt & eliminates odors. This is a task that takes some time, and we at Xpress Shine Motocare, do so with utmost care and quality materials. Keeping your vehicle’s carpet, upholstery & other surfaces clean can keep your car’s interior looking great & in good shape.

Car Washing

A quick washing and drying of the car exterior Under chassis wash Cleaning of the superficial surface of your rims Application of tire dressing to the exterior surface of your tires Clean windows inside & out Dust and wipe all the accessible hard surfaces inside including the front dash, centre console, door panels, etc Thoroughly vacuum the seats, interior carpeting & 4 floor mats

Waxing/Polishing

Intensive 3 step compound polishing/buffing process for painted surfaces. A polymer based wax is applied to the seal & protect the polished areas.

Towing Services

In case of breakdown/accident cases, where vehicle is not in running condition, we shall assist towing your car to a workshop.

Puncture

We have a 24/7 mobile puncture unit.

Jumper Start

In case of battery breakdown, we shall assist you in getting your vehicle started & in running condition at your place.

About Us

We are open 8am to 1am past midnight, including Sundays. Sit back and relax while we cater to redefining the shine of your vehicle!
